I am a freelance UX/UI Designer. I specialize in app and web design and excel in content strategy. I enjoy finding new ways to touch and improve people’s everyday lives with assistive technology. I have a wide variety of skills in user interface and user experience design and also dabble in coding. Check out my Skills and Work to see more! My Design Principle Accomplishments I am driven by creating designs that generate valuable experiences focusing on user-centric solutions that build on the principles of accessibility and usability. My ultimate goal is to create user value with innovation. A great thinker and designer knows that there is always occasion to gain more knowledge and insight in their given field. I delight in the idea of always being a student with the chance to grow and learn from my peer interactions and collaborations. I truly love ideating and tinkering with a concept and prototype and continuously improving it. Just like with the opportunity of gaining knowledge, there is always an opportunity to improve an idea or design and take a solid product from great to essential.
